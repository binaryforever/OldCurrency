Old Currency

Privacy Policy — Last updated April 2026

Old Currency is a historical currency rate lookup app developed by Vincent Holmes. This privacy policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Data We Do Not Collect

Old Currency does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any other identifying information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs
  • We do not use cookies or any form of tracking
  • We do not store any data on our own servers

Network Requests

When you request a currency rate, the app makes a network call to the Frankfurter open-source currency data API to retrieve the exchange rate for the selected currencies and date. The only information sent in this request is the currency codes and date you have selected — no personal or device information is included. Please refer to the Frankfurter website for their own data practices.

Third-Party Services

Old Currency uses the Frankfurter API as its sole external data source. No other third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect data are used.

Children's Privacy

Old Currency does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the App Store listing for Old Currency.
